About

Ravid Iny is an Interior Designer and Project Manager based in Milan, with experience across residential, hospitality and retail projects throughout Europe and the United States.
His work combines a strong design sensibility with a practical understanding of how projects are developed, coordinated and delivered. From private residences and hospitality environments to international retail spaces, he has worked across all stages of the design process, from concept development and spatial planning to technical detailing, consultant coordination and construction support.

Having collaborated with multidisciplinary teams on projects for both independent clients and global brands, Ravid approaches each project with a balance of creativity, precision and attention to detail. He is particularly interested in creating spaces that feel considered, functional and enduring, where architecture, materials and atmosphere come together to support the way people live, work and interact..
