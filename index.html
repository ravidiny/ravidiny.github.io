About

Ravid Iny is an Interior Designer and Project Manager based in Milan, working across residential, hospitality, retail and commercial environments.
His practice combines concept development, spatial planning, technical coordination and custom furniture design. With international experience across Europe and the United States, his work is guided by materiality, proportion and atmospheric detail.
